Status and category label chip — Futura Bold, uppercase, 0px border-radius.

```jsx
<Badge variant="yellow">Nuevo</Badge>
<Badge variant="electric">Oferta</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">Inline</Badge>
<Badge variant="accent" dot>En Stock</Badge>
<Badge variant="error" dot>Agotado</Badge>
<Badge variant="success" dot>Disponible</Badge>
```

**Variants:** `default` (dark fill) · `yellow` (brand yellow) · `electric` (neon lime — high urgency) · `outline` (white border) · `accent` (yellow border + text) · `error` / `success` (status)

**Sizes:** `sm` 9px · `md` 10px (default) · `lg` 11px

**dot prop:** Adds a leading 5px circle in `currentColor` — useful for stock/availability status.
